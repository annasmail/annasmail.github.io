Pushing with Magnets

Can magnets be used to ‘push’ magnetic nanoparticles?

In this project, the hypothesis can magnets be used to ‘push’ magnetic nanoparticles was examined. For two identical magnets, a Python code was developed to calculate their resultant magnetic field. It was dependent on parameters such as distance between magnets and their relative rotations. Experimental analysis was then conducted to determine the code's accuracy.
